Hi! I'm HARMEET KAUR

I am Harmeet Kaur, a dedicated educator with 14 years of experience in training students and teachers in Abacus, Vedic Maths, and Robotics. As a life coach, I guide individuals in overcoming their challenges and unlocking their full potential.

My passion lies in helping students overcome their fear of mathematics and discover the joy of learning. Through my coaching, students achieve remarkable growth in speed, accuracy, and confidence in their mathematical abilities.

Each year, my students participate in three prestigious competitions: Inter-State, National, and International levels. These competitions bring together over 4,000 students at a time, making it a unique and enriching experience for everyone involved.

I take pride in supporting my students who struggle with mathematics by helping them overcome their fear and excel in their activities. My goal is to make learning math a fun and empowering journey for every student.
